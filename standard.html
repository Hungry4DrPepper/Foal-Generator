About

The Foal Generator was created to generate random Sims 3 foals for roleplay purposes. Sometimes the surprise of how a foal turns out is just the magic you need to connect with that horse. On this site there are two generators available, the Standard Generator and the Surprise Generator The standard generator takes you to a page where you can quickly get a randomized foal! See their gender, phenotype, genotype, and everything else you'd need to know. Some may say this takes away the fun of anticipation though... that's why there is also a complete surprise option. Go to the surprise generator for a more slow paced generation where you can individually randomize each aspect instead of all at once.





Planned features

  • Randomized phenotype that corresponds with genotype
  • Randomized sex (filly or colt)
  • Randomized markings, including head, body, and legs
  • Randomized dilutions and modifiers depending on parent's genetics
  • Ability to save foal information to Local Storage (further ahead)
